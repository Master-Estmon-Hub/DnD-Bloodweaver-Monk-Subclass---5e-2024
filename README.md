# D-D-Homebrew---Antares-Subclasses
Homebrew 5e 2024 Subclases for FoundryVTT
- Fighter Subclass: Karak'Thari Gunslinger. A highly customizable subclass with multiple different playstyles for those who want a more complex fighter. You'll be able to build the gunslinger you want, a sniper, a dual gunner, a heavy blaster, a bombardier or a combination of those.
- Monk Subclass: Warrior of the Crimson Heart. The ultimate bloodweaver, a monk capable of using its own blood to create blood weapons, recover HP and Focus Points, perform secret bloodweaving techniques, sense the heartbeats of living creatures and even empower allies or weakening enemies.
